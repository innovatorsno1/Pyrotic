Classic Idli

Classic Idli is a soft and fluffy steamed rice cake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. Itâ€™s a perfect breakfast or snack option, often paired with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (preferably idli rice)
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (skinned black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Water (for soaking and grinding)
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds (if using) in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Drain and grind the soaked ingredients to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Let the batter ferment for 8-12 hours or overnight in a warm place.
  4. Once fermented, add salt and stir gently.
  5. Grease the idli molds and pour the batter into each mold.
  6. Steam the idlis for 10-12 minutes until cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
