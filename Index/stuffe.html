Stuffed Idli

Stuffed idli is a delicious twist on the classic idli, where the idlis are filled with a spiced vegetable or potato filling, making them more flavorful and filling.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • For filling: 2 boiled potatoes, 1 onion, 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon mustard seeds

Instructions:

  1. Soak and grind rice and urad dal into a smooth batter, ferment it overnight.
  2. Prepare the filling by saut√©ing onions, mustard seeds, and turmeric, then mix in the mashed potatoes.
  3. Pour batter into idli molds, add a spoonful of the filling in the center, and cover with more batter.
  4. Steam the idlis for 10-12 minutes until cooked through.
